Program reminders

  1. Organization contact persons must clearly indicate their intent to participate in the ACC referral program through email.
  2. Each organization must use only one contact person and one organization email address; non-compliance may invalidate referrals associated with excess entries.
  3. The designated contact person must maintain a verified ShopeePay account and an active Shopee username for program verification.
