What is Shopee's After Class Club?

Shopee Campus Challenge 2026 gives organizations a clear chance to drive student sign-ups, deliver practical campus value, and compete for meaningful rewards within a defined campaign window.

As a core campaign incentive, organizations receive 50 pesos per validated referral after meeting the required quota.

What students get from After Class Club

  • Instant welcome rewards and practical value students can use every day.
  • VIP partner perks
  • Daily Shopee Pabaon
  • Order missions that convert activity into rewards

What are the rewards for participating organizations?

A tiered incentive structure based on validated referral performance.

  • Base Reward: Minimum of 50 validated referrals, 50 pesos per validated referral.
  • Gold Tier: Minimum of 100 validated referrals, 4,500 pesos in vouchers.
  • Diamond Tier: 200 to 300 validated referrals, 8,500 pesos in vouchers.
  • Grand Champion: Top leaderboard performer, 20,000 pesos in vouchers and an exclusive networking session with Shopee.

Timeline

July 27, 2026: Pre-registration submissions commence. August 3, 2026: Official validation and referral tracking begin. October 3, 2026 (11:59 PM): The campaign concludes.
