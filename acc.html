What is Shopee's After Class Club?

ACC is a student-first digital survival toolkit designed to make campus life lighter, smarter, and more rewarding.

ACC is no longer just about occasional discounts. It gives students everyday support through practical savings, repeatable perks, and reward opportunities that match real campus routines from weekdays to crunch weeks.
